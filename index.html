What started as a small startup has grown into the Netherlands' most trusted train ticket platform, serving over 100,000 happy travelers annually.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance can I book tickets?

You can book tickets up to 6 months in advance. For the best deals, we recommend booking at least 3 months before your travel date.

What is your cancellation policy?

We offer free cancellation up to 24 hours before departure. Cancellations within 24 hours may be subject to a small fee.

Are there senior or student discounts?

Yes! We offer special rates for seniors (60+), students, and youth travelers (under 26). Valid ID required at time of travel.

How do I get my tickets?

Tickets are delivered instantly via email and our mobile app. You can choose to print them or show the digital version during your journey.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All transactions are secure and encrypted.
